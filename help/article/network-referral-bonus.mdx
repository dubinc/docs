---
title: "Dub Network referral bonus"
description: "Learn how the Dub Network referral bonus works and how you can share your referral link to start earning."
---

Dub offers a Network referral bonus that lets you earn rewards when you invite other partners to join the Dub partner network and they start earning commissions on Dub.

When another partner joins Dub through your referral link, you can earn **50% of your referred partners' payout fees for their first 12 months**.

## How to access the Network Referrals page

You can access the Network Referrals page via this permalink: [partners.dub.co/referrals](https://partners.dub.co/referrals)

Alternatively, you can also click on the gift icon in the bottom left corner from the main navigation:

## Sharing your referral link

On the Network Referrals page, copy your referral link and share it with partners you want to invite to Dub.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/network-referral-link.png"
    alt="Network referral link"
  />
</Frame>

Alternatively, you can also link directly to any program on Dub by appending your Dub username to the end of the link.

For example, if your Dub username is `steven` and you want to link to [Dub's affiliate program](https://partners.dub.co/dub), you can share the following link: [partners.dub.co/dub/apply?via=steven](https://partners.dub.co/dub/apply?via=steven)

Then, when someone signs up through your link, Dub attributes that partner and all their future commissions for the first 12 months to your Dub partner account.

<Note>
  If the program you're linking to already has a [Partner Referral
  reward](/help/article/partner-referrals) that you're eligible for, the Partner
  Referral reward will take precedence over the Dub Network Referral bonus.
</Note>

## Tracking referrals and earnings

The Dub [Network Referrals page](https://partners.dub.co/referrals) shows:

- **Referred partners** – the number of partners who joined Dub through your referral link
- **Your earnings** – the referral bonus earnings attributed to your network referrals
- **Referral rewards** – the current network referral reward terms
- **Referred partner details** – the partner's join date, location, active programs, and your earnings from that partner

## Getting paid

Network referral rewards are paid through your Dub partner payout account.

To receive your referral rewards, make sure you have [connected your payout method](/help/article/receiving-payouts). Once your rewards are eligible for payout, they follow the same payout process as your other Dub partner earnings.

Dub offers a free trial so you can explore [Pro features](https://dub.co/pricing) before committing to a paid plan. The trial gives your workspace full access to Pro-tier functionality, subject to the [trial limits](#free-trial-limits) below.

## Starting a trial

<Note>
  There is a limit of one free trial per workspace.
</Note>

1. From your workspace dashboard (e.g. `app.dub.co/your-workspace`), click on the **Settings** tab.
2. On the left sidebar, click on **Billing**.
3. Click **Start free trial** to activate your trial.

Once activated, your workspace is immediately upgraded to Pro-tier limits for the duration of the trial.

## Free trial limits

The trial follows the same general behavior as a paid Pro plan, with the following usage caps:

| Resource                  | Free trial limit |
| ------------------------- | ---------------- |
| Links                     | 100              |
| Clicks                    | 5,000            |
| Payouts                   | $10,000          |
| Domains                   | 5                |
| Tags                      | 5                |
| Folders                   | 5                |
| Groups                    | 5                |
| Network invites           | 0                |
| Users                     | 5                |
| AI credits                | 100              |
| API rate limit            | 120 req/min      |
| Analytics API rate limit  | 2 req/min        |
| Partners                  | 100              |

To monitor your workspace's current usage, head to **Settings → Billing** in your dashboard.

Once your usage reaches 100% of any of the limits above, the trial will be paused until you upgrade to a paid plan or the next billing cycle begins.

## Post-trial decision

Your trial finishes after the trial period ends or once your workspace exceeds the usage limits, whichever happens first. After which, you can opt for one of two paths:

- [Upgrade to a paid plan](#upgrade-to-a-paid-plan)
- [Revert to the Free plan](#revert-to-the-free-plan)

### Upgrade to a paid plan

To continue using Pro features after your trial, you'll need to add a payment method so your workspace transitions seamlessly from the trial to a paid plan.

To add a payment method:

1. From your workspace dashboard, click on the **Settings** tab.
2. On the left sidebar, click on **Billing**.
3. Click **Open billing portal** to add or update your payment method in Stripe.

Billing begins immediately after your trial ends if you have added a payment method. See [updating billing information](/help/article/how-to-change-billing-information) for more details.

### Revert to the Free plan

Without a payment method, your workspace automatically reverts to the [Free plan](https://dub.co/pricing) when the trial ends. All Free plan limits will apply, and any resources over the Free plan limits (e.g. extra links, domains, or users) may become read-only until you're back within the limits.

You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime later by visiting **Settings → Billing** and adding a payment method.

  This feature is only available on [Business plans and
  above](https://dub.co/pricing/partners).
</Tip>

Commission analytics helps you analyze your partner program's commission earnings over time. It is useful when you want to understand payout trends, compare commission activity across a date range, or see which partners and referral activity are driving earnings.

## Where to find commission analytics?

When viewing your Partner Program navigation, click on the **Analytics** page in the **Insights** group.

In the analytics chart, select **Commissions** from the top data selector.

## Commission analytics views

The commission analytics dashboard uses filtering and date range controls like the [program analytics](/help/article/program-analytics), but focuses the chart on commission earnings.

### 1. Commission chart

The commission chart shows commission earnings across the selected date range. You can use it to identify spikes, dips, and trends in partner payouts.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/program-analytics-timechart-commissions.png"
    alt="Time-series chart"
  />
</Frame>

**Data toggle**

You can change the data displayed by clicking the `$ | 123` toggle in the top right of the data type section.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/program-analytics-datatoggle-commissions.png"
    alt="Time-series chart"
  />
</Frame>

By default, `$` is selected to show commission value. Switching to `123` changes the chart to show the number of commissions instead.

### 2. Partner commission data

Below the chart, you can review partner-level commission performance for the selected period. This helps you identify which partners are generating the most commission activity.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/program-analytics-partnertable-commissions.png"
    alt="Partner data table"
  />
</Frame>

To inspect individual commission records, go to the [Commissions page](/help/article/partner-commissions).

### 3. Aggregated data for different facets (top views)

These are more commonly known as the "Top Views" in Dub Analytics. These views show your top **Partner Groups** and commission **Types**, and allow you to explore your program data further.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/program-analytics-topviews-commissions.png"
    alt="Aggregated data for different facets"
  />
</Frame>

## Filtering commission analytics

You can click the **Filter** button in the top left to narrow commission analytics by partner, group, and commission type.

The date picker controls the reporting period for the commission chart and partner data.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/program-analytics-filter-commissions.png"
    alt="Program analytics location"
  />
</Frame>

| Filter    | Description                                                                                          |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Partner   | Select a specific program partner                                                                    |
| Partner Group     | Select a specific partner group                                                                      |
| Commission type | Select a specific commission type (eg: click, lead, sale)                                      |

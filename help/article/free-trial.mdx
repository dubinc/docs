Dub offers a free trial so you can explore additional features before committing to a paid plan. The trial gives your workspace full access to paid plan features, subject to the [trial limits](#trial-limits) below.

<Note>
  To prevent abuse, we currently only offer one free trial per workspace.
</Note>

## Trial limits

To prevent abuse, we apply additional usage limits during your trial period for certain resources:

| Resource                                                                            | Trial limit |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------- |
| 👥 [**Dub Partners**](https://dub.co/partners)                                      |             |
| [Partners](/help/article/dub-partners)                                              | 50         |
| [Partner Groups](/help/article/partner-groups)                                      | 5           |
| [Payouts](/help/article/partner-payouts)                                            | $500/mo     |
|                                                                                     |             |
| 🔗 [**Dub Links**](https://dub.co/links)                                            |             |
| [Links](/help/article/dub-links)                                                    | 100/mo      |
| [Analytics](/help/article/dub-analytics)                                            | 5,000/mo    |
| [Domains](/help/article/how-to-add-custom-domain)                                   | 5           |
| [Tags](/help/article/how-to-use-tags)                                               | 5           |
| [Folders](/help/article/link-folders)                                               | 5           |
|                                                                                     |             |
| 🏛️ **Other resources**                                                              |             |
| [Teammtaes](/help/article/how-to-invite-teammates)                                  | 5           |
| [API rate limit](/docs/api-reference/rate-limits)                                   | 120 req/min |
| [Analytics API rate limit](/docs/api-reference/rate-limits#rate-limits-by-endpoint) | 2 req/min   |

<Tip>
  To monitor your workspace's current usage, head to [**Settings →
  Billing**](https://app.dub.co/settings/billing) in your dashboard.
</Tip>

Once your usage reaches 100% of any of the limits above, you will need to upgrade to a paid plan to unlock full access to your plan's limits.

## Trial emails

When you start a free 14-day trial on Dub, we will send you the following emails:

- **Trial started**: The first email confirming your trial has started, along with a few resources to help you get started.
- **Mid-trial check-in**: A mid-trial check-in email to see how you're doing and if you need any help.
- **3 days before trial ends**: A final reminder email 3 days before your trial ends.

## What happens after the trial ends?

Once your trial ends, your card will be automatically charged for the first month of your paid plan.

If you don't want to proceed with the paid plan, you can cancel your subscription any time during your trial. See [canceling your subscription](/help/article/how-to-cancel-subscription) for more details.

If you want to expand your partnerships or join new programs on Dub, this guide walks you through how to browse available programs, review their details, and submit an application.

## Where to find the Program Marketplace

From **Your Programs** navigation, click **Marketplace**.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/program-marketplace-nav.png"
    alt="Program Marketplace in the navigation"
  />
</Frame>

Here you can browse featured programs, marketplace categories, and programs in each category to learn more about and [apply to](/help/article/program-marketplace#applying-to-a-program).

## All programs
To view all programs in the marketplace, click **View all programs** or select a category from the list. This view will show you all the Dub programs available to apply to, along with their reward types and categories.

<Frame><img src="/images/dub-partners/marketplace-all-programs.png" alt="All available programs"/></Frame>

On the program cards, you'll get a quick overview of the program, including the rewards offered, the program categories, a brief description of the company, and your [enrolment status](/help/article/program-marketplace#enrolment-status). Hover over some of the details to see more information.

<Frame><img src="/images/dub-partners/marketplace-card-details.png" alt="Program card details"/></Frame>

Click the program card to view the full details of the [program](/help/article/program-marketplace#viewing-program-details).

### Filtering
You can filter the programs availble by their reward type, category, and [enrolment status](/help/article/program-marketplace#enrolment-status).

<Frame><img src="/images/dub-partners/marketplace-filtering.png" alt="Marketplace filtering"/></Frame>


### Sorting
You can also sort the results by most popular, newest, or alphabetical order. By default the grid is sorted by most popular, giving a sense of what programs to apply to first.

<Frame><img src="/images/dub-partners/marketplace-sorting.png" alt="Marketplace sorting"/></Frame>

### Viewing program details

To view details about a program, click any program card.

<Frame><img src="/images/dub-partners/program-marketplace-details.png" alt="Program details"/></Frame>

The program details page includes:

- Product description
- Rewards offered
- Program eligibility requirements if you don't meet them to apply
- Program categories
- Additional supporting content to help you understand the partnership

## Joining the Dub Partner Network

Before you can apply to programs in the marketplace, your [partner profile](https://partners.dub.co/profile) needs to be accepted into the Dub Partner Network.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/network-tasks.png"
    alt="Dub partner network acceptance tasks"
  />
</Frame>

To submit your Dub Partner Network application, complete all 6 tasks in your [profile page](https://partners.dub.co/profile):

- Upload your logo
- Verify at least 2 social accounts/website
- Fill out your profile description
- Specify your estimated monthly traffic
- Select your preferred earning structures
- Choose your sales channels

Once all tasks are complete, click **Submit application**. Your application will be reviewed by the Dub team.

| Status    | Description                                                                                                                  |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Submitted | Your Dub Partner Network application is under review. You will be able to apply to marketplace programs once it is approved. |
| Approved  | You can apply to programs in the marketplace and get discovered by brands in the Dub Partner Network.                        |
| Rejected  | Your Dub Partner Network application was not approved. Continue improving your profile and reapply in the future.            |

<Tip>
  A complete, verified profile helps programs understand who you are, where you
  reach your audience, and which partnerships are the best fit for you.
</Tip>

## Applying to a program

After your Dub Partner Network application is approved, click the **Apply** button in the top right of the Program details page.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/program-apply.png"
    alt="Program apply button"
  />
</Frame>

Each program has its own application and enrollment requirements. Fill out the form as accurately and honestly as possible to increase your chances of approval.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/program-application.png"
    alt="Program application"
  />
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Before applying, make sure your profile is up to date and as complete as
  possible. [Learn more](/help/article/partner-profile)
</Tip>

After completing the form, click **Submit application**. You will be notified by email once your application is approved.

## Enrolment status
The program details page and card view will show your enrolment status for each program in the marketplace. 

| Status    | Description |
| --- | --- |
| No status | You have not applied to this program yet. |
| Enrolled | You are approved and enrolled in the program. |
| Applied | Your application is under review. You will be notified by email once your application is approved. |
| Rejected | Your application was not approved. The program may also decide if you can reapply in the future. |
| Not eligible | You don't meet the eligibility criteria for this program and cannot apply. |
| Invited | You have been invited to the program, and can accept the invitation without needing to apply. |
| Declined | You have declined the invitation to join the program. You can reapply in the future if you change your mind. |
| Deactivated | You have been removed from the program by the program owner. |
| Banned | You have been banned from the program by the program owner. |

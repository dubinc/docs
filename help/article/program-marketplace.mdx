If you want to expand your partnerships or join new programs on Dub, this guide walks you through how to browse available programs, review their details, and submit an application.

## Where to find the Program Marketplace

From the **All Programs** navigation, click **Marketplace**.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/program-marketplace-nav.png"
    alt="Program Marketplace in the navigation"
  />
</Frame>

Here you can browse featured programs and a full list of programs available to learn more about and [apply to](/help/article/program-marketplace#applying-to-a-program).

## Filters and sorting

Within the program grid, you can refine what you see by applying filters or sorting options. This helps narrow programs based on categories or the reward types available.

You can filter by several criteria, then sort the results by most popular, newest, or name order.

To view details about a program, click any program card.

<Frame>
  <img

- Product description
- Rewards offered
- Program categories
- Additional supporting content to help you understand the partnership

## Dub Network Acceptance

Before you can apply to programs in the marketplace, your profile needs to be accepted into the Dub Network.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/dub-partners/network-tasks.png"
    alt="Dub network acceptance tasks"
  />
</Frame>

To submit your Dub Network application, complete all 6 profile tasks:

- Upload your logo
- Verify at least 2 social accounts/website
- Fill out your profile description
- Specify your estimated monthly traffic
- Select your preferred earning structures
- Choose your sales channels

Once all tasks are complete, click **Submit application** from your profile. Your application will be reviewed by the Dub team.

| Status | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Submitted | Your Dub Network application is under review. You will be able to apply to marketplace programs once it is approved. |
| Approved | You can apply to programs in the marketplace and get discovered by brands in the Dub Network. |
| Rejected | Your Dub Network application was not approved. Continue improving your profile and reapply in the future. |

<Tip>
  A complete, verified profile helps programs understand who you are, where you
  reach your audience, and which partnerships are the best fit for you.
</Tip>

## Applying to a program

After your Dub Network application is approved, click the **Apply** button in the top right of the Program details page.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/marketplace-apply.png"
    alt="Program apply button"
  />
</Frame>

Each program has its own application requirements. Fill out the form as accurately as possible to increase your chance of approval.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/marketplace-application-2.png"
    alt="Program application"
  />
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Before applying, make sure your profile is up to date and as complete as
  possible. [Learn more](/help/article/partner-profile)
</Tip>

After completing the form, click **Submit application**. You will be notified by email once your application is approved.
